Dirty Chai

Description

A dirty chai is a mix of chai tea, milk and coffee (the ingredient that makes it "dirty").

Ingredients:

  • 6oz chai concentrate (such as Oregon Chai)
  • 6oz of milk (alternative milks work fine as well)
  • Instant coffee (read package for quantity)

Directions:

  1. Heat milk in a small pot over low heat
  2. Mix in instant coffee
  3. Add milk coffee mix to mug with chai concentrate
  4. (Optional) Instead of heating the milk the mixture can be served over ice!
