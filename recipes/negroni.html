Negroni

Description

A classic bitter cocktail consisting of equal parts campari, vermouth and gin.

Ingredients:

  • 1oz Gin
  • 1oz Campari
  • 1oz Sweet vermouth
  • Garnish: Orange peel

Directions:

  1. Add gin, campari and vermouth to a rocks glass filled with ice.
  2. Stir till glass is chilled and condensation begins to form.
  3. Rub orange peel against rim and garnish to preference.
